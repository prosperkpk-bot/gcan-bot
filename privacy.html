Privacy Policy — GCAN-Bot

GCAN-Bot is a WhatsApp assistant for Grace Church of All Nations (GCAN), operated by ArtVentures. When you message this bot, your message text and phone number are processed to generate a relevant response about church services, prayer requests, giving, ministries, or related topics.

Messages are sent securely to Anthropic (the provider of the Claude AI model) solely to generate a reply, and are not sold or shared with any other third party. We do not use your information for marketing or share it with other churches or businesses using this service.

If you have questions about this policy or wish to have your information removed, contact GCAN directly at gcan@gmail.com or +267 71 548 786.
